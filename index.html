Quick Chef


Welcome to your new bakery! It's the gand opening and customers are lined up to order. The next 5 min will be critical to your future success. Complete orders to make as much money as you can to become the best restaurant on the block!


  1. Use the number pad [1]-[6] to select ingredients.
  2. Use the [left], [right] and [up] arrow keys to move and pickup items.
  3. Use the [down] arrow key to drop what you are holding.
  4. Collect items by dropping them in the Assembly Station to the left.
  5. Use the [up] arrow to pick-up items from the Assembly Station.
  6. Use [a],[w],[s],[d] to combo 4 key to cook held items.
  7. Serve orders by dropping them in the Serving Station to the right.
